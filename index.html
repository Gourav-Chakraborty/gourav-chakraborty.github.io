About Me

I am a Computational Biophysicist specializing in Molecular Dynamics, Machine Learning, and DFT calculations. My research focuses on deciphering complex protein-protein interactions—with a particular emphasis on Intrinsically Disordered Proteins (IDPs) and their role in Parkinson's Disease. By leveraging ML-based Enhanced Sampling, I try to uncover cryptic conformational states that drive disease pathogenesis.

This site serves as a central repository for my research. You’ll find my latest publications, various open-source project and my current work in molecular simulations.
